About us

An autonomous RC racing initiative founded by computer science and engineering students aiming to develop intelligent interactive autonomy in scale RC cars. Using techniques in imitation learning, distillation, model-based reinforcement learning to enable the race car to learn interpretable representations of the world, predict future trajectories, and choose the best course of action, acting intelligently against competitors and preventing unwanted events.
